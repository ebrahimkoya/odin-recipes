Description

Known as the worlds most famous chai, Ebrahim's chai has long been sought after. Finally, after many decades, it is being released to the public so that all may enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea Bags
  • Milk
  • Optional: Sugar

Steps

  1. Fill up pot with water, aim for 250 ml per cup
  2. Place the pot on the stove, wait till it starts to boil
  3. Insert one tea bag for each cup of chai, a tea spoon of sugar per cup as well if you are using sugar
  4. When it starts to boil again, pour in milk. Aim for a deep orange colour
  5. Let the chai sit on the stove till it starts to boil again
  6. Turn off the stove and let it cool down at this point0
  7. Enjoy!
