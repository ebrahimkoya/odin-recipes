Description

While Ebrahim is renowned for his chai, he also has started his coffee journey. Starting with a simple keurig brewer, he now prefers to hand grind his coffee beans and use an aeropress to brew his daily cup of coffee!

Ingredients

  • Coffee Beans (Preferably medium roast)
  • Water
  • An Aeropress
  • A kettle
  • A scale
  • Optionally: Sugar, milk or creamer

Steps

  1. Weigh out 16g of coffee (if using whole bean, grind it)
  2. Brew the kettle untill the water starts boiling
  3. Assemble the Aeropress onto the mug, placing the coffee grinds into it
  4. Pour in 200g of the nearly boiling water into the aeropress, ensuring that all coffee grinds are immersed
  5. Place the Aeropress plunger into the chamber, creating a vaccum that stops coffee from pouring into the mug
  6. Let the coffee grinds rest for two minutes or longer
  7. When ready, Push the plunger through the chamber, extracting your coffee into the mug
  8. Finish with creamer and sugar if you so choose
  9. Enjoy!
